F.A.Q.

WHEN SHOULD I GO TO URGENT CARE?
Whenever you have an unexpected illness or injury that is non-life threatening, and your primary care doctor is not available, we encourage you to visit 1st Choice Urgent Care. We’re here 365 days/year – 9 a.m to 9 p.m. seven days/week, and 9 a.m. – 5 p.m on holidays.
